Why Atomic is Flagged as Malware
Atomic Executor is flagged as Win64/Xgen by some antivirus programs because it injects into Roblox, which is a common behavior for executors. This is a false positive. It only edits Roblox files to enable scripting and does not harm your system. Compare this to other popular executors like Atlantis (60k+ users), which are also flagged but widely trusted. Don't call us a rat—this is how executors work!
